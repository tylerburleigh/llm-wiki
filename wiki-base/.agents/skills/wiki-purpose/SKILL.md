# wiki-purpose

Use when reviewing or refining the human-owned `purpose.md`.

## Workflow

- Read `purpose.md`, `wiki/synthesis.md`, `wiki/index.md`, and recent
  `wiki/log.md` entries.
- Compare the stated purpose against what the wiki has accumulated.
- Propose focused edits to goal, questions, scope, and thesis.
- Ask for approval before editing `purpose.md`.

## Boundaries

- The human owns the research direction.
- Do not invent a new purpose from wiki contents alone.
- Do not ingest, query, repair, or lint as part of purpose review unless
  the user asks.
